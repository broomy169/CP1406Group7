Goals

Goals: The new website must attract new members, increase member engagement, as well as increase event ticket sales and be easier to navigate, read and maintain.

Success Evaluation

Success Evaluation: By calculating the sum gained in memberships compared to the year before, at the time the site has been established the user can evaluate the success of the website.

Target Audience

Target Audience: The target audience that the site is intended for are individuals that have a passion for music and want to be part of a group. Generally, these individuals would be approximately 20-40 years of age.

Content

  • Note: All pages contain the same navigation bar at the top of the page, and a footer at the bottom of the page.
  • Home - Facebook feed and description of relevant site content.
  • About Us - Suitable Image, Description of Orchestra, Table consisting of Management Committee, History of Barrier Reef Orchestra
  • Gallery - Will include photos of performers and past events in a free flowing slideshow.
  • News - Information about upcoming events and performers
  • Events - Information about past and upcoming events
  • People - Shows management and committee members, life members with pictures and descriptions
  • Supporters - Information and pictures of clientel and sponsors
  • Contact Us - Form for User to contact orchestra for information/sign-up
